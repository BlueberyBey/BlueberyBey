LENIENCY

LENIENCY

Leniency is a personal project that merges poetry with illustration, following the journey of a knight in search of his mother while confronting his deepest fears. Drawing from dark-fantasy aesthetics, the project conveys silence, melancholy, and resilience.

I used cold and dark color palettes to establish a somber mood, while applying contrast and white space to separate characters and landscapes from their backgrounds. This visual approach creates a strong interplay between illustration and poetry, enhancing both the narrative clarity and emotional depth of the project.
